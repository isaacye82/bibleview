Revelation 14: The Lamb & The Harvest

锡安的羔羊与地上的收割

The Lamb on Zion and the Harvest of the Earth

在第十三章令人压抑的兽的统治之后，第十四章展现了得胜的景象：羔羊与144,000人站在锡安山上。随后，三位天使发出最后的警告，宣告巴比伦的倾倒和对拜兽者的审判。这一章以两个收割异象结束：庄稼的收割（得救者）和葡萄的收割（受审者），预示着历史的终局。

After the oppressive reign of the beasts in Chapter 13, Chapter 14 presents a vision of victory: the Lamb standing on Mount Zion with the 144,000. Three angels then issue final warnings, proclaiming the fall of Babylon and judgment on beast-worshippers. The chapter concludes with two harvests: the grain (saved) and the grapes (judged), signaling the end of history.

收割全地

锡安山 (Mount Zion) Place of Security

与13章兽的权势形成鲜明对比。锡安山代表神永不动摇的国度。144,000人在这里不仅是受保护的，更是得胜、纯洁并跟随羔羊的军队。

Contrasts with the Beast's power. Zion represents God's unshakable kingdom. The 144,000 here are protected, victorious, pure, and following the Lamb.

三天使信息 The 3 Angels' Messages

末世最后的福音宣告与警告：1. 永远的福音（敬畏神）；2. 巴比伦的倾倒（拒绝世界体系）；3. 拜兽者的结局（喝神忿怒的酒）。

Final end-time warnings: 1. Everlasting Gospel (Fear God); 2. Fall of Babylon (Reject world system); 3. Doom of Beast worshippers (Wrath of God).

酒醡 (Winepress) Wrath of God

葡萄熟透了（罪恶满盈），被丢入神忿怒的大酒醡中。这象征着末日对恶人彻底、剧烈且不可逆转的审判。

Grapes are fully ripe (sin is full), thrown into the winepress of wrath. Symbolizes the thorough, violent, and irreversible judgment of the wicked.

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于象征语言（童身、锡安山、酒醡）的旧约背景（约珥书、以赛亚书）及启示录内部的对比（兽的印记 vs 神的名）。
Focusing on symbolic language (virgins, Zion, winepress), OT background (Joel, Isaiah), and internal contrasts (Mark of Beast vs Name of God).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    将144,000理解为神子民的完整象征（而非字面数字）。“童身”指属灵的贞洁（未沾染偶像），而非对婚姻的否定。收割异象预示末世的双重结局。
    Interpret 144,000 as symbolic completeness. "Virgins" means spiritual purity (no idolatry), not celibacy. Harvest visions signal the dual end-time destiny.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于在诱惑中保持纯洁、在逼迫中持守忍耐，以及对死亡的正确态度（息了劳苦）。
Focusing on purity amidst temptation, endurance in persecution, and the correct attitude toward death (rest from labor).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    跟随羔羊不仅是在天堂，更是地上的生活方式。在“巴比伦”的文化中保持“童身”的纯洁。
    Following the Lamb is a lifestyle on earth, not just in heaven. Keeping "virgin" purity amidst "Babylonian" culture.

"圣灵说：是的，他们息了自己的劳苦，做工的果效也随着他们。"

"'Yes,' says the Spirit, 'they will rest from their labor, for their deeds will follow them.'"
