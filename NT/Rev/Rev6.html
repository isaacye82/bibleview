Revelation 6: The Seals of Judgment

羔羊的忿怒与印的开启

The Wrath of the Lamb and the Opening of Seals

当第五章的赞美声渐弱，第六章通过羔羊揭开七印中的前六印，将场景转向了审判。这章展示了历史上战争、饥荒、死亡的残酷现实，以及受造界的最终动摇。这是神公义的彰显，也是对罪恶世界的拆毁。

As the praises of Chapter 5 fade, Chapter 6 shifts to judgment as the Lamb opens the first six seals. This chapter reveals the brutal realities of war, famine, and death in history, culminating in the cosmic shaking of creation. It is the manifestation of God's justice and the dismantling of a sinful world.

The 5th seal shows the perspective of the suffering church. "How long?" is a cry for justice, not mere revenge.

第六印预示了“主的大日”。它解构了受造界的秩序，使得无论是君王还是奴隶都无处可藏。

The 6th seal anticipates the "Great Day of the Lord," deconstructing creation's order so that neither king nor slave can hide.

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于解析启示文学的象征语言、旧约背景及原文含义，避免过度臆测。
Focusing on decoding apocalyptic symbolism, OT background, and original meaning, avoiding speculation.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    关注颜色的象征（白、红、黑、灰）和旧约（如撒迦利亚书）的对应。区分“羔羊的忿怒”与世俗的灾难。
    Focus on color symbolism and OT parallels (e.g., Zechariah). Distinguish "Wrath of the Lamb" from secular disasters.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于在审判的异象中反思个人的信仰状态，以及如何在动荡中站立得稳。
Reflecting on personal faith amidst visions of judgment, and how to stand firm in turmoil.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    不要只看灾难的可怕，要看到神对罪恶的不妥协。问自己：“在那日我能站立得住吗？”
    Don't just see the horror of disaster; see God's uncompromising stance on sin. Ask: "Can I stand on that day?"
