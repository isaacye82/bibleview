Revelation 5: The Scroll & The Lamb

天上宝座前的危机与解答

The Crisis and Solution in the Throne Room

第五章是第四章天上敬拜异象的延续。这里出现了一个戏剧性的张力：代表神历史计划的书卷是封严的，全宇宙无人配展开。谁有权柄执行神的旨意？答案揭示了基督信仰的核心悖论——狮子即是羔羊。

Chapter 5 continues the vision of the heavenly throne room. A dramatic tension arises: God holds a scroll representing His plan for history, but it is sealed. Who has the authority to execute God's will? The answer reveals the central paradox of the Christian faith—the Lion is the Lamb.

Revelation 5:5

犹大支派的狮子

Lion of the Tribe of Judah

大卫的根 Root of David

他已得胜 Has Triumphed!

书卷 Biblion (The Scroll)

代表地球的所有权契约，或神审判与救赎的计划。里外都写着字，表明内容的完整性与不可更改性。

Represents the title deed to the earth or God's redemptive plan. Written on both sides, indicating completeness and immutability.

狮子 Leon (The Lion)

应许中的弥赛亚君王。这是约翰“听见”的形象，象征着神圣的权柄和对罪恶的终极审判。

The promised Messianic King. This is what John "heard", symbolizing divine authority and ultimate judgment over sin.

被杀的羔羊 Arnion (The Slain Lamb)

这是约翰“看见”的形象。只有通过牺牲的爱，神才能在不违背公义的前提下救赎罪人。

This is what John "saw". Only through sacrificial love can God redeem sinners without violating His justice.

Methodology of Interpretation and Application

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于经文的历史背景、原始语言和神学架构，旨在挖掘作者的原始意图。
Focusing on historical context, original language, and theological structure to excavate the author's original intent.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    关注历史背景（如罗马遗嘱的七印封严）和希腊原文含义（如 Biblion, Arnion）。
    Focus on historical context (e.g., Roman wills with 7 seals) and Greek meanings.
  • 本章应用 Application
    理解基督作为“得胜者”的合法性，以及敬拜在宇宙秩序中的中心地位。
    Understanding the legitimacy of Christ as the "Victor" and the centrality of worship in the cosmic order.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于将经文真理内化为个人的生命体验，回应心灵的渴望与挣扎。
Focusing on internalizing scriptural truth into personal life experience, responding to the heart's longings and struggles.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    在“狮子与羔羊”的张力中寻找安慰，学习在软弱中依靠神的大能。
    Finding comfort in the tension of "Lion and Lamb", learning to rely on God's power in weakness.
  • 本章应用 Application
    当生命看似“封严”时，通过赞美和祷告（金炉）参与神的计划。
    Participating in God's plan through praise and prayer (golden bowls) even when life seems "sealed".

整合运用 Integration

“正确的理解引向正确的敬拜。我们研读经文（头脑）是为了点燃心灵（情感）。”

"Correct understanding leads to correct worship. We study the text (Head) to ignite the heart (Affection)."

"曾被杀的羔羊是配得权柄、丰富、智慧、能力、尊贵、荣耀、颂赞的！"

"Worthy is the Lamb who was slain, to receive power and wealth and wisdom and strength and honor and glory and praise!"
