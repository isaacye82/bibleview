启示录第十二章

Revelation 12: The Woman, The Child & The Dragon

宇宙的争战：妇人、龙与男孩子

Cosmic Conflict: The Woman, The Dragon, and The Male Child

第十二章拉开了启示录下半部分的序幕，揭示了历史背后的属灵真相。这不是新的时间线，而是对历史深层维度的揭示。我们看到了一个“大异象”：一位身披日头的妇人（神的子民），一条大红龙（撒但），以及一位被提的男孩子（基督）。这章讲述了从基督诞生到末世，善恶之间永恒的战争。

Chapter 12 opens the second half of Revelation, unveiling the spiritual reality behind history. This is not a new timeline, but a revelation of the deep dimension of history. We see a "great sign": a Woman clothed with the sun (God's people), a Red Dragon (Satan), and a Male Child (Christ). It narrates the eternal war between good and evil from Christ's birth to the end times.

身披日头的妇人

The Woman Clothed with Sun

Israel & The Church
以色列与教会

妇人 (The Woman) Israel / Church

象征神在旧约（以色列，生出弥赛亚）和新约（教会，受逼迫）中的子民。日头、月亮、十二星呼应约瑟的梦，指向以色列十二支派。

Symbolizes God's people in OT (Israel, birthing Messiah) and NT (Church, persecuted). Sun, moon, 12 stars echo Joseph's dream, pointing to the 12 tribes.

大红龙 (The Dragon) Satan / Ancient Serpent

也就是魔鬼撒但。七头十角象征其在世上的巨大权势和破坏力。红色象征杀戮。它时刻准备吞吃那个将要出生的孩子。

The Devil/Satan. 7 heads/10 horns symbolize immense worldly power and destructiveness. Red symbolizes slaughter. He waits to devour the Child.

男孩子 (Male Child) Christ the Messiah

这是弥赛亚耶稣，将用铁杖辖管万国。祂的被提（升天）标志着龙彻底失败的开始。

Messiah Jesus, who will rule nations with an iron scepter. His being "snatched up" (Ascension) marks the beginning of the Dragon's total defeat.

解经与应用的方法论

Methodology of Interpretation and Application

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于识别象征符号（妇人、龙、男孩子）的旧约根源（创世记3章、37章）和神学意义。
Focusing on identifying the OT roots (Gen 3, 37) and theological meaning of symbols (Woman, Dragon, Child).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    避免将妇人仅限于马利亚，而应理解为神子民的整体（以色列+教会）。从“天上的视角”解读地上的冲突。
    Avoid limiting the Woman to Mary only; understand her as the collective People of God. Interpret earthly conflict from a "Heavenly Perspective".

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于属灵争战的得胜之道：不是靠武力，而是靠羔羊的血和见证的道。
Focusing on the way of victory in spiritual warfare: not by force, but by the blood of the Lamb and the word of testimony.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    要在生活的每一个“旷野”时期看到神的供应（被养活），在控告者（撒但）面前宣告基督的义。
    See God's provision in every "wilderness" season; declare Christ's righteousness before the Accuser.

"弟兄胜过它，是因羔羊的血和自己所见证的道。他们虽至于死，也不爱惜性命。"

"They triumphed over him by the blood of the Lamb and by the word of their testimony; they did not love their lives so much as to shrink from death."
