Revelation 10: The Mighty Angel & The Scroll

不再耽延：先知的使命与神的奥秘

No More Delay: The Prophetic Mission and the Mystery of God

第十章是第六号与第七号之间的插曲。一位大力的天使从天降下，宣告“不再耽延了”。这一章将焦点从对世界的审判转向了先知本身：约翰被命令吃下展开的小书卷。这象征着重新接受先知的使命——品尝神话语的甜蜜，也承担其审判的苦涩。

Chapter 10 serves as an interlude between the 6th and 7th Trumpets. A mighty angel descends, declaring "There will be no more delay." The focus shifts from judgment on the world to the prophet himself: John is commanded to eat the open little scroll. This symbolizes a recommissioning—tasting the sweetness of God's Word while bearing the bitterness of its judgment.

大力的天使 The Mighty Angel

身披云彩，头上有虹，脸面像日头。这极可能是荣耀基督的显现（Christophany）或祂的特使。踏海踏地象征对全地的主权。

Clothed in a cloud, rainbow over head, face like the sun. Likely a Christophany or His special envoy. Standing on sea and land implies total dominion.

七雷 (封印) Seven Thunders (Sealed)

唯一被明确禁止记录的启示。这表明神有隐秘的事（申29:29），我们也应接受神主权的保留，不需知道所有细节。

The only revelation explicitly forbidden to be written. Shows God has secret things (Deut 29:29); we must accept His sovereign reservation.

吃书卷 Eating the Scroll

先知必须将神的话“消化”成为生命的一部分。口中甜（因是神的话），腹中苦（因是审判的信息）。

The prophet must "digest" God's Word into his being. Sweet in mouth (God's truth), bitter in stomach (message of judgment).

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于旧约背景的呼应（以西结书的吃书卷、但以理书的封印）以及天使身份的辨析。
Focusing on OT parallels (Ezekiel's scroll, Daniel's sealing) and identifying the Angel.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    将“大力的天使”的特征与基督（启1章）对比。理解“不再耽延”为神奥秘成就的时刻已到，而非字面时间的停止。
    Compare "Mighty Angel" features with Christ (Rev 1). Understand "No more delay" as the fulfillment of mystery, not end of literal time.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于信徒与神话语的关系：不仅是理性的阅读，更是生命的消化与承担。
Focusing on the believer's relationship with God's Word: not just intellectual reading, but digestion and bearing.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    接受真理的双重性：救恩的甘甜与审判的沉重。鼓励在不明白（七雷）时信靠神的主权。
    Accepting the duality of truth: sweetness of salvation & weight of judgment. Encouraging trust in God's sovereignty when things are hidden (7 thunders).
