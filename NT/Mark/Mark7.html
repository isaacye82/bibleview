Mark 7: Tradition vs. Heart

外在的传统与内在的洁净

Outward Tradition and Inner Purity

第七章是关于“洁净”观念的革命性教导。耶稣挑战了法利赛人关于洗手、饮食和器皿的繁琐遗传，直指问题的核心：人心的污秽。祂宣告“从外面进去的不能污秽人，唯有从里面出来的乃能污秽人”。随后，耶稣通过在外邦地区的神迹（推罗西顿的妇人、耳聋舌结者），表明救恩跨越了种族界限，真正的不洁不在于血统或食物，而在于不信与刚硬。

Chapter 7 presents a revolutionary teaching on "purity". Jesus challenges the elaborate traditions of hand-washing and diet, pointing to the core issue: the human heart. He declares that what goes in does not defile, but what comes out does. Through miracles in Gentile regions (Syrophoenician woman, Deaf-Mute), He shows salvation crosses ethnic lines; true uncleanness lies in unbelief, not food or lineage.

古人的遗传 Tradition of the Elders

法利赛人将口传律法（Mishnah）提升到与圣经同等的地位。耶稣批评他们“废弃神的诫命，遵守人的遗传”。

Pharisees elevated oral law (Mishnah) to Scripture's level. Jesus critiqued them for "setting aside God's commands to observe human traditions."

人心的污秽 Defilement of the Heart

真正的污染源不是不洗手或不洁净的食物，而是从心里发出的恶念。这是对犹太洁净礼仪彻底的重新定义。

True pollution isn't unwashed hands or food, but evil thoughts from the heart. A radical redefinition of Jewish purity laws.

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于犹太背景（各耳板、洁净礼）、耶稣对旧约的引用（以赛亚书）及地理转移（推罗、西顿、低加波利）。
Focusing on Jewish context (Corban, purity laws), OT citations (Isaiah), and geographical shifts (Tyre, Decapolis).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    分辨神圣律法与人为传统的区别。理解耶稣废除饮食条例的神学意义（为向外邦人传福音铺路）。
    Distinguish Divine Law from Human Tradition. Understand theological significance of declaring all foods clean (opening Gospel to Gentiles).

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于省察内心的隐秘动机、对宗教形式主义的反思，以及学习谦卑的信心。
Focusing on examining inner motives, reflecting on religious formalism, and learning humble faith.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    不仅要洗手，更要洁心。不满足于嘴唇的尊敬，追求心与神的亲近。即使被拒绝（如希腊妇人），仍坚持信靠。
    Wash heart, not just hands. Seek heart-closeness, not just lip-service. Persist in faith even when tested/rejected (like the Greek woman).
