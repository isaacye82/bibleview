Mark 16: Resurrection & Commission

祂已经复活了
He Has Risen!

"Do not be alarmed... He is not here."

马可福音以一个震撼宇宙的空坟墓作为高潮。在七日的第一日清晨，悲伤的妇女们发现石头已经滚开，死亡的权势已被打破。第16章不仅宣告了耶稣的复活，更发出了将这好消息传遍天下的伟大差遣。尽管原文在第8节有不同的结尾版本，但整章的核心信息是明确的：基督得胜，信者蒙福，福音广传。

Mark's Gospel climaxes with a universe-shaking empty tomb. Early on the first day, grieving women found the stone rolled away and the power of death broken. Chapter 16 not only declares the Resurrection but issues the Great Commission to spread this good news. Despite textual variations, the core message remains: Christ is Victor, believers are blessed, and the Gospel spreads.

空坟墓 The Empty Tomb

巨大的石头已经滚开，坟墓空了。天使宣告“祂不在这里”。这是基督教信仰的基石——死亡不能拘禁生命的主。

The stone rolled away; the tomb empty. "He is not here." Death could not hold the Lord of Life.

大使命 The Great Commission

“你们往普天下去，传福音给万民听。”复活不仅是历史事件，更是行动的号角，呼召门徒将好消息带给每一个受造物。

"Go into all the world." Resurrection is a call to action, sending disciples to every creature.

升天与同工 Ascension & Co-working

主被接到天上，坐在神的右边。但故事并未结束，主与门徒“一同工作”，用神迹随着证实所传的道。

He sat at God's right hand. Yet He worked with them, confirming the word by signs.

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于处理文本批判问题（长结尾与短结尾）、复活的历史证据（空坟墓、妇女见证）、以及大使命的神学含意（洗礼、神迹）。
Focusing on textual criticism (Long/Short endings), historical evidences (Empty tomb, Women witnesses), and theology of the Commission.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    理解在父权社会中女性作为首批见证人的真实性。平衡理解“信而受洗”与“神迹随着”的应许。
    Authenticity of women witnesses in patriarchal society. Balanced view of "believe and be baptized" and "signs following".

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于从惊恐到信心的转变（不要惊恐）、从静止到行动的跨越（去！）、以及对主同在的经历（主和他们同工）。
Focusing on transformation from fear to faith, the leap from stillness to action ("Go!"), and experiencing His presence ("Lord working with them").

  • 核心原则 Principles
    不仅相信复活的事实，更活出复活的大能。不将信仰私有化，而是顺服“往普天下去”的命令。
    Not just believing the fact, but living the power of Resurrection. Not privatizing faith, but obeying the command to "Go".

"主和他们同工，用神迹随着，证实所传的道。"

"The Lord worked with them and confirmed his word by the signs that accompanied it."
