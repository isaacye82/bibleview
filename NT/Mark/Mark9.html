Mark 9: Glory, Faith & Discipleship

山上的荣耀与山下的现实

Glory on the Mountain and Reality in the Valley

第九章呈现了强烈的对比：在变像山上，耶稣显出神性的荣耀，摩西和以利亚显现，父神亲自见证；而在山下，门徒却因信心不足无法赶鬼，陷入争论。本章的核心在于如何在看见荣耀后，回到现实生活中跟随受苦的基督，学习“为首的要作仆人”的真理。

Chapter 9 presents a stark contrast: on the Mount of Transfiguration, Jesus reveals divine glory, Moses and Elijah appear, and the Father testifies; while in the valley, disciples fail to cast out a demon due to lack of faith and fall into dispute. The core theme is how to follow the suffering Christ in daily reality after seeing the glory, learning that "the first must be servant of all."

Listen to Him

登山变像 The Transfiguration

预尝天国的权能。摩西（律法）和以利亚（先知）的出现，证明耶稣是启示的终极成全。父神命令：“你们要听他！”

A foretaste of Kingdom power. Moses (Law) & Elijah (Prophets) attest Jesus as the ultimate fulfillment. The Father commands: "Listen to Him!"

祷告的信心 Faith through Prayer

“非用祷告，这一类的鬼总不能出来。”门徒的失败源于依靠过去的经验而非当下的倚靠。信心是接通神能力的管道。

"This kind can come out only by prayer." Disciples failed by relying on past experience, not present dependence. Faith channels God's power.

十字架的道路 Way of the Cross

耶稣第二次预言受难。门徒却在争论谁为大。真正的伟大在于做众人的仆人，甚至接待一个小孩子。

Jesus predicts His death a 2nd time. Disciples argue who is greatest. True greatness lies in being a servant of all, even welcoming a child.

解经与应用的方法论

Methodology of Interpretation and Application

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于马可的对比叙事（山上的荣耀 vs 山下的失败）、旧约背景（以利亚、摩西）及夸张修辞（砍手、挖眼）的理解。
Focusing on Mark's contrasting narratives (Glory vs Failure), OT background (Elijah, Moses), and hyperbole (cutting hand/eye).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    将“变像”视为神国降临的预尝。理解“地狱”（Gehenna）的历史背景。将“盐”解释为圣约的忠诚或净化的功能。
    See Transfiguration as a foretaste of the Kingdom. Understand historical context of "Gehenna". Interpret "Salt" as covenant loyalty or purification.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于信心的操练（我信，但我信不足）、谦卑的学习（谁为大）及对罪的决绝态度。
Focusing on exercising faith ("I believe, help my unbelief"), learning humility (who is greatest), and radical stance against sin.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    诚实面对信心不足，并向主求助。在教会生活中实践“接待小孩”般的谦卑。对隐秘的罪采取“断臂”般的果断。
    Admit weak faith and ask for help. Practice humility by welcoming the "child" (insignificant). Be radical against secret sin.
