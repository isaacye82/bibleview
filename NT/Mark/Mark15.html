Mark 15: The Crucifixion & Death

十字架上的君王
The King on the Cross

"Surely this man was the Son of God!"

马可福音第15章记载了人类历史上最黑暗也是最荣耀的一天。从彼拉多的审判庭到骷髅地的十字架，耶稣在沉默中承受了世人的羞辱与神的忿怒。当祂断气之时，殿里的幔子裂开，百夫长承认祂是神的儿子。这一章不仅是受难的记录，更是神救赎计划的最高潮。

Mark 15 chronicles the darkest yet most glorious day in history. From Pilate's court to Golgotha, Jesus bore shame and wrath in silence. As He breathed His last, the temple curtain tore, and the centurion confessed Him as God's Son. This is the climax of redemption.

伟大的交换 The Great Exchange

强盗巴拉巴被释放，无罪的耶稣被定罪。这是福音的核心画面：罪人得释放，义人替罪受死。

Barabbas the rebel is released; Jesus the innocent is condemned. A picture of the Gospel: the guilty freed, the Righteous One dies in their place.

黑暗时刻 The Hour of Darkness

遍地黑暗，耶稣大声喊叫“我的神！为什么离弃我？”祂独自承受了神对罪恶的忿怒，经历了与父神隔绝的终极痛苦。

Darkness covered the land. Jesus cried, "My God, why have you forsaken me?" He bore God's wrath and separation alone.

幔子裂开 The Torn Curtain

耶稣断气时，圣殿的幔子从上到下裂为两半。通往至圣所的路已通过祂的身体打开，神与人之间的阻隔被除去。

The temple curtain tore from top to bottom. The way to the Holy of Holies is open through His sacrifice; the barrier is removed.

解经与应用的方法论

Methodology of Interpretation and Application

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于受难叙事中的旧约应验（诗篇22篇、以赛亚书53章）、罗马的刑罚背景（鞭打、十字架）、以及神学象征（黑暗、裂开的幔子）。
Focusing on OT fulfillment (Psalm 22, Isaiah 53), Roman context (scourging, crucifixion), and theological symbols (darkness, torn curtain).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    将“被神离弃”理解为背负罪孽的替代性审判。分析百夫长承认“神的儿子”在外邦宣教中的重要性。
    Understand "forsakenness" as substitutionary judgment. Analyze the Centurion's confession for Gentile mission.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于对十字架大爱的凝视（看这个人）、对罪恶的痛悔（是我的罪钉死祂）、以及对神主权的顺服（虽然不明，仍旧信靠）。
Focusing on gazing at the Cross ("Behold the Man"), repentance for sin ("my sin nailed Him"), and submission to Sovereignty.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    在“我的神，为什么离弃我”的呼喊中找到我们得救的确据。效法亚利马太的约瑟，在危机中勇敢跟随。
    Find assurance in His cry of forsakenness. Imitate Joseph of Arimathea's courage in crisis.
