马可福音第六章

Mark 6: Rejection, Mission & Miracles

被弃绝的先知与大能的供应者

The Rejected Prophet and The Mighty Provider

第六章是马可福音中最长的一章，充满了强烈的对比。耶稣在家乡拿撒勒被厌弃，却在旷野通过五饼二鱼喂饱五千人；希律王在宫殿里斩了施洗约翰，耶稣却在海面上行走显明神性。本章展示了即使在人心的不信和政治的逼迫下，神国度的扩展和怜悯的供应依然不可阻挡。

Chapter 6, the longest in Mark, is full of contrasts. Jesus is rejected in Nazareth but feeds 5000 in the wilderness; Herod beheads John, while Jesus walks on water. It shows that despite unbelief and persecution, the Kingdom's expansion and merciful provision are unstoppable.

Divine Provision
神圣供应

拿撒勒的厌弃 Rejection at Nazareth

“先知在本地本家...没有不被尊敬的。”乡亲因熟悉耶稣的肉身背景（木匠）而感到冒犯（Scandalized），这限制了他们的信心和神迹的发生。

"A prophet is not without honor except in his own town." Familiarity bred contempt. Offense at his humanity limited their faith and miracles.

差遣十二使徒 Sending the Twelve

耶稣赐权柄给门徒，两个两个地差遣出去。这标志着事工的扩展：从耶稣一人做，到门徒团队做。轻装上阵的原则教导对神的完全倚靠。

Jesus empowers and sends them 2 by 2. Ministry expands from One to Many. Traveling light teaches total dependence on God.

动了慈心 Moved with Compassion

这是五饼二鱼神迹的动机。耶稣视众人如同“没有牧人的羊”。神迹不仅是能力的展示，更是神怜悯心肠的流露。

Motivation for feeding the 5000. Jesus saw them as "sheep without a shepherd." Miracles flow from divine compassion, not just power.

解经与应用的方法论

Methodology of Interpretation and Application

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于马可的叙事对比（Rejection vs Sending）、旧约预表（五饼二鱼与旷野吗哪、以利沙的倍增）及神显（Theophany，水上行走）。
Focusing on Markan narrative contrasts (Rejection vs Sending), OT typology (Manna, Elisha), and Theophany (Walking on Water).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    将约翰之死视为耶稣受难的预演。理解“我是”（Ego Eimi）的神性宣称。
    View John's death as a preview of Jesus' passion. Understand "I Am" (Ego Eimi) as a divine claim.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于面对拒绝时的态度、在缺乏中信靠神的供应，以及在风浪中认出主的同在。
Focusing on attitude in rejection, trusting God's provision in lack, and recognizing His presence in storms.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    不要因熟悉而轻看主。将你的“五饼二鱼”交在主手中。
    Don't let familiarity breed contempt. Place your "loaves and fishes" in His hands.

"放心！是我，不要怕！"

"Take courage! It is I. Don't be afraid."
