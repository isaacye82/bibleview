Mark 14: The Passion Narrative

苦杯与亲吻
The Cup and The Kiss

"Abba, Father... take this cup from me. Yet not what I will, but what you will."

马可福音第14章是受难叙事的开端。这是漫长而黑暗的一夜：从伯大尼那极昂贵的香膏，到最后晚餐的新约；从客西马尼园的痛苦挣扎，到犹大诡诈的亲吻。在这里，我们看到人性的极致软弱（门徒的背叛与逃跑）与神性的极致顺服（耶稣的“不要照我的意思”）形成了最强烈的对比。

Mark 14 marks the beginning of the Passion narrative. It is a long and dark night: from the costly perfume at Bethany to the New Covenant at the Last Supper; from the agonizing struggle in Gethsemane to Judas' treacherous kiss. Here, we see the starkest contrast between the ultimate weakness of humanity (the disciples' betrayal and flight) and the ultimate submission of divinity (Jesus' "not what I will").

打破玉瓶 Extravagant Worship

在耶稣安葬之前，一位女子打破玉瓶，献上极珍贵的香膏。这是对主毫无保留的爱，与犹大的贪婪形成对比。

Before Jesus' burial, a woman broke an alabaster jar and offered very precious perfume. This is unreserved love for the Lord, in stark contrast to Judas' greed.

设立圣餐 The Last Supper

耶稣赋予逾越节全新的意义。饼是祂的身体，杯是祂立约的血。这标志着神与人之间新约的开始。

Jesus gives Passover a new meaning. The bread is His body, and the cup is His blood of the covenant. This marks the beginning of the New Covenant.

客西马尼 Gethsemane

在这黑暗的时刻，耶稣独自承受了对罪与死亡的极度惊恐，最终在祷告中完全顺服父的旨意。

In this dark hour, Jesus alone bore the extreme horror of sin and death, ultimately submitting completely to the Father's will in prayer.

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于受难叙事的历史细节（逾越节习俗）、神学意义（立约的血、人子）、以及文学对比（玛利亚的奉献 vs 犹大的背叛）。
Focusing on Passion narrative details (Passover), theological significance (Blood of Covenant), and literary contrasts (Mary vs Judas).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    理解“阿爸，父”在亚兰文中的亲密性与独特性。分析“人子”在审判席前宣告的弥赛亚权柄。
    Understand intimacy of "Abba". Analyze the Messianic authority of "Son of Man" before the Sanhedrin.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于对主爱的回应（打破玉瓶）、在试炼中的顺服（不要照我的意思）、以及对软弱的悔改（彼得的眼泪）。
Focusing on responding to love (breaking the jar), submission in trial ("not my will"), and repentance from weakness (Peter's tears).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    在黑暗时刻学习“警醒祷告”。面对失败（如彼得）时，不绝望，而是仰望主的怜悯。
    Learn to "watch and pray" in dark hours. Facing failure, do not despair but look to His mercy.
