Mark 5: Power Over Demons, Disease & Death

超越一切的权柄：鬼魔、疾病与死亡

Authority Over All: Demons, Disease, and Death

第五章通过三个震撼人心的神迹，展示了耶稣对人类最深重困境的绝对统治权：被群鬼辖制的人（精神与灵界的捆绑）、患血漏的妇人（长期疾病与社会隔离）以及睚鲁的女儿（死亡的终极绝望）。无论面对何种绝境，耶稣的回应都是：“不要怕，只要信。”

Chapter 5 showcases Jesus' absolute dominion over humanity's deepest predicaments through three stunning miracles: the demon-possessed man (spiritual bondage), the woman with bleeding (chronic disease and isolation), and Jairus' daughter (the finality of death). In every desperate situation, Jesus' response remains: "Don't be afraid; just believe."

群鬼 (Legion) Spiritual Bondage

一个被“群”（罗马军团编制，约6000）鬼附着的人，住在坟茔里，无人能制伏。耶稣一句话就释放了他，显明神国度对撒但国度的全面胜利。

A man possessed by "Legion" (Roman unit ~6000), living in tombs, uncontrollable. Jesus freed him with a word, showing total victory over Satan.

信心的触摸 (Touch of Faith) Touch of Faith

患血漏的妇人虽在律法上不洁，却因信心触摸耶稣的衣裳繸子而得医治。耶稣称她为“女儿”，不仅医治身体，更恢复了她的社会身份。

The bleeding woman, ritually unclean, touched Jesus in faith and was healed. Jesus called her "Daughter", restoring both health and dignity.

睡着了 (Sleeping) Victory Over Death

面对死亡的终极绝望，耶稣重新定义死亡为“睡觉”。“大利大，古米”（闺女，起来）展现了生命之主温柔而绝对的唤醒能力。

Facing death's finality, Jesus redefines it as "sleeping". "Talitha koum" (Little girl, get up) shows the gentle, absolute power of the Lord of Life.

解经与应用的方法论

Methodology of Interpretation and Application

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于马可的叙事技巧（“三明治结构”：睚鲁-妇人-睚鲁）、地理背景（外邦人的低加波利）及神学象征（不洁与洁净的逆转）。
Focusing on Markan narrative technique (Sandwich structure), geography (Decapolis), and theological reversal of clean/unclean.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    理解耶稣不仅是犹太人的弥赛亚，也是外邦人的主。注意耶稣对“触摸”禁忌的突破。
    Understand Jesus as Lord of Gentiles too. Note His breaking of "touch" taboos.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于绝望中的信心、对耶稣能力的信靠，以及被医治后的见证生活。
Focusing on faith in desperation, trust in Jesus' power, and the witness life after healing.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    在看似绝望的环境中听到主说“不要怕，只要信”。学习像被鬼附者得释放后那样，回家传扬主恩。
    Hear "Don't be afraid" in despair. Learn to testify at home like the freed man.
