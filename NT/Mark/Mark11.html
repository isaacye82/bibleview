Mark 11: Judgment & Authority

君王的审判与权柄
The King Has Come

马可福音第11章标志着耶稣事工的最后一周（受难周）的开始。耶稣以弥赛亚君王的身份公开进入耶路撒冷，随即对虚伪的宗教体制（无花果树与圣殿）施行了严肃的审判。本章充满了关于权柄、果子与信心的深刻教导。

Mark 11 marks the beginning of the final week (Passion Week) of Jesus' ministry. Jesus publicly enters Jerusalem as the Messianic King, immediately executing solemn judgment on the hypocritical religious system (the fig tree and the Temple). This chapter is filled with profound teachings on authority, fruitfulness, and faith.

荣入圣城 The Triumphal Entry

耶稣骑驴进城，应验了撒迦利亚的预言。这是和平之君的加冕，而非军事征服者。众人的欢呼（和散那）既是欢迎也是误解。

Jesus enters on a colt, fulfilling prophecy. A coronation of the Prince of Peace, not a warlord.

无花果树与圣殿 Fig Tree & Temple

马可的三明治结构：咒诅无花果树夹着洁净圣殿。只有叶子没有果子的树，象征着只有宗教外表却无祷告实质的圣殿。

Mark's sandwich structure. The leafy but fruitless tree symbolizes the busy but prayerless Temple.

移山的信心 Mountain-Moving Faith

面对枯干的树，耶稣教导对神的信心。真正的信心是在祷告中相信神的大能，并活出饶恕的生命。

Jesus teaches faith in God. True faith believes in God's power in prayer and lives out forgiveness.

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于马可的“三明治结构”（无花果-圣殿-无花果）、旧约背景（万国祷告的殿）及象征性行动（咒诅与洁净）的理解。
Focusing on Mark's "Sandwich Structure", OT background (House of Prayer), and symbolic actions (Cursing & Cleansing).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    将无花果树视为以色列/圣殿的象征。理解耶稣的怒气是针对宗教的商业化与虚伪。将“移山”置于祷告与信心的语境中。
    See fig tree as symbol of Israel/Temple. Understand Jesus' anger against religious commercialism. Contextualize "Moving Mountains" in prayer.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于省察生命的果子（外表 vs 实质）、敬拜的纯洁性（祷告的殿 vs 贼窝）及信心的实践（饶恕与祷告）。
Focusing on fruitfulness (appearance vs reality), purity of worship, and the practice of faith (forgiveness & prayer).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    省察我的信仰是否只有“叶子”。我的心是祷告的殿还是交易的市场？在祷告中不仅求能力，更求饶恕的心。
    Check if my faith is only "leaves". Is my heart a house of prayer or a market? Seek forgiveness in prayer, not just power.
