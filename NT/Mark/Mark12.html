Mark 12: Wisdom, Conflict & Sacrifice

交锋与智慧
The Cornerstone Rejected

在第12章，耶稣在圣殿中面对了一波又一波来自宗教领袖的挑战。通过一系列精彩的辩论（纳税、复活、最大的诫命），耶稣不仅展示了属天的智慧，更确立了自己作为“匠人所弃的石头”却是神国基石的地位。本章以穷寡妇的奉献作结，将虚假的宗教外表与真实的内心奉献进行了鲜明对比。

In Mark 12, Jesus faces wave after wave of challenges from religious leaders in the temple. Through a series of brilliant debates (Taxes, Resurrection, Greatest Commandment), Jesus not only displays divine wisdom but establishes Himself as the "stone the builders rejected" which has become the cornerstone. The chapter concludes with the widow's offering, contrasting false religious appearance with true inner devotion.

房角石

The Cornerstone

True Authority
真实的权柄

葡萄园的审判 Parable of Tenants

耶稣用比喻揭示了宗教领袖对神的悖逆：他们不仅杀害仆人（先知），最后还要杀害爱子（耶稣）。审判将临到他们。

Jesus reveals the leaders' rebellion: they killed the prophets and finally the Son. Judgment is coming.

爱神与爱人 The Greatest Command

在一切诫命中，耶稣总结了核心：尽心爱神，并爱人如己。这超越了一切燔祭和祭祀，是天国伦理的基石。

The core of the law: Love God with all your heart, and neighbor as yourself. This surpasses all sacrifices.

完全的奉献 The Widow's Offering

与文士虚伪的长袍相比，穷寡妇投入的两个小钱显出了真正的虔诚。神不看数量，看的是牺牲的程度。

Contrasted with the scribes, the widow's two mites show true piety. God measures sacrifice, not amount.

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于比喻的象征（园户、石头）、拉比式的辩论（该撒的物、复活）、以及对当时宗教伪善的社会学分析。
Focusing on parabolic symbolism (tenants, stone), rabbinic argumentation (Caesar's things, resurrection), and sociological analysis of religious hypocrisy.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    理解“房角石”在诗篇118篇中的弥赛亚含意。分辨“神的形象”在纳税问题中的神学深度。分析“尽心”在示玛（Shema）祷告中的整全性。
    Understand "Cornerstone" messianic meaning (Ps 118). Discern "Imago Dei" in the tax question. Analyze wholeness of "Shema".

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于全人的投入（尽心爱主）、身份的归属（神的物归神）以及奉献的真诚（两个小钱）。
Focusing on total commitment (Love with all heart), identity/belonging (God's things to God), and sincerity of giving (Two mites).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    省察我是否在管家的职分上忠心。我的生命是否铭刻着神的形象？我的奉献是出于富余，还是出于生命的全部？
    Examine stewardship faithfulness. Is God's image imprinted on my life? Is my giving out of surplus or out of substance?
