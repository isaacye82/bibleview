Song of Songs 1: The Royal Wedding

歌中的雅歌
The Song of Songs

雅歌第1章揭开了神圣罗曼史的序幕。这不仅仅是一首关于所罗门与书拉密女的爱情诗，更是基督（良人）与教会（新妇）之间亲密关系的预表。在这里，我们看到渴望、寻找、自我认知的挣扎，以及王对新妇无条件的肯定。

Song of Songs Chapter 1 unveils the prologue of a divine romance. It is not merely a love poem between Solomon and the Shulammite, but a type of the intimate relationship between Christ (the Beloved) and the Church (the Bride). Here we see longing, seeking, the struggle of self-identity, and the King's unconditional affirmation.

"Let him kiss me... for your love is more delightful than wine." The Bride expresses deep longing.

The Bride realizes her weakness (dark), yet she is precious in the King's eyes (lovely).

解经与应用的方法论

Hermeneutics: Interpretation & Application

经文含义 Scriptural Meaning

定义： 这种方法侧重于挖掘经文的历史背景、文学体裁（希伯来诗歌）和原文词义。对于《雅歌》，我们首先将其视为表达人类神圣爱情的文学作品，同时也承认其作为神与子民关系的预表性（Typology）。

Definition: Focuses on historical context, genre (Hebrew poetry), and original language. For Song of Songs, we view it primarily as sacred human love literature, while acknowledging its typology of God and His people.

灵修默想 Spiritual Devotion

定义： 这种方法将经文的真理内化为个人的属灵体验。在《雅歌》中，我们将“良人”视为基督，将“新妇”视为信徒的灵魂，寻求在个人关系中回应神的爱，建立亲密的内在生活。

Definition: Internalizes truth into personal spiritual experience. In Song of Songs, we see the "Beloved" as Christ and the "Bride" as the believer's soul, seeking to respond to God's love in intimacy.
