Song of Songs 2

The Winter is Past

雅歌第2章是一首关于生命复苏与亲密关系深化的赞歌。从“沙仑的玫瑰”到“爱的旗帜”，再到良人呼唤新妇“起来，与我同去”，本章描绘了在基督里的属灵春天。

Song of Songs Chapter 2 is a hymn of revived life and deepening intimacy. From the "Rose of Sharon" to the "Banner of Love," and the Beloved's call to "Arise and come away," this chapter portrays the spiritual springtime in Christ.

Spring of Life
爱的旗帜 Banner of Love

他在我以上打爱之旗。这是公开的归属、保护与夸耀。

His banner over me is love. A public declaration of belonging, protection, and pride.

冬天已往，雨水止住。良人呼唤新妇走出内室，进入丰盛的生命。

Winter is past. The Beloved calls the Bride out of hiding into abundant life.

Remove the small sins and compromises that ruin the vineyard of intimacy.

解经与应用的方法论

Hermeneutics: Interpretation & Application

正统解经 Authoritative Exegesis

原则： 侧重于原文（希伯来文）的字义研究、历史背景（所罗门时代的植物、地理、风俗）以及文学体裁（情诗与预表）的分析。避免过度的寓意解经，首先尊重经文的字面意义——即人类爱情的神圣性。

Principle: Focuses on original Hebrew, historical context (flora, geography), and literary genre. Respects the literal meaning of sacred human love first.

灵修应用 Spiritual Application

原则： 在正确理解字义的基础上，将“良人与新妇”的关系应用到“基督与信徒”或“基督与教会”的属灵关系中。强调个人与神之间亲密的互动、对神之爱的回应以及内在生命的成长。

Principle: Applies the "Beloved-Bride" relationship to "Christ-Believer". Emphasizes personal intimacy, response to divine love, and inner growth.

"良人属我，我也属他。"

"My beloved is mine and I am his."
