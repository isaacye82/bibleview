雅歌第四章

The Enclosed Garden

关锁的园，封闭的泉
A Garden Enclosed

雅歌第4章是良人对新妇最长、最细腻的赞美。从对她身体每一个细节的称颂（眼、发、牙、唇），到邀请她离开危险的利巴嫩山，最终将她比作一座“关锁的园”。这章展示了基督眼中信徒的完美，以及神对我们专属之爱的渴慕。

Chapter 4 is the Beloved's most detailed praise of the Bride. From extoling her physical beauty (eyes, hair, teeth, lips) to inviting her away from Lebanon's dangers, he ultimately describes her as a "garden enclosed." This reveals the believer's perfection in Christ's eyes and God's desire for exclusive love.

Myrrh Aloes Calamus
全然美丽 Altogether Beautiful

良人连用七个比喻赞美新妇，宣告“你全然美丽，毫无瑕疵”。这是恩典视角下的信徒。

The Beloved uses seven metaphors to praise her, declaring "no flaw in you." The believer viewed through grace.

关锁的园 Garden Enclosed

新妇不再是“未看守的葡萄园”（1:6），而是专属良人的私密花园，只有祂能进入享受。

No longer a "neglected vineyard," she is now a private garden for the Beloved alone. Exclusive devotion.

北风与南风 North & South Winds

新妇主动邀请风（圣灵）吹进园子，无论是顺境还是逆境，只为散发出香气供良人享受。

Inviting the winds (Spirit) to blow—whether trial or comfort—so fragrance may flow for the King.

园中的香气 Fragrance of the Garden

没药 (Myrrh) - 受苦 Suffering 乳香 (Frankincense) - 祷告 Prayer 番红花 (Saffron) - 信心 Faith 沉香 (Aloes) - 破碎 Brokenness 活水 (Living Water) - 圣灵 Spirit
这些香料代表了圣灵在信徒生命中结出的各样果子。
These spices represent the varied fruits of the Spirit in a believer's life.

解经与应用的方法论

Hermeneutics: Interpretation & Application

正统解经 Authoritative Exegesis

原则： 侧重于原文（希伯来文）的字义研究（如Wasf赞美诗体裁）、地理背景（基列山、利巴嫩）和文化习俗。理解良人对新妇身体各部位的描述并非单纯的情色描写，而是古代近东表达尊贵与完美的诗意语言。

Principle: Focuses on genre (Wasf), geography (Gilead, Lebanon), and culture. Understands the physical descriptions as poetic expressions of dignity and perfection, not mere erotica.

灵修应用 Spiritual Application

原则： 将“关锁的园”等意象应用到信徒的内心世界。强调基督对信徒生命的鉴赏、信徒对世界的分别为圣（关锁），以及圣灵（风）在生命中产生果效的必要性。

Principle: Applies imagery like "Garden Enclosed" to the inner life. Emphasizes Christ's delight in the believer, separation from the world, and the Spirit's work.

"愿我的良人进入自己园里，吃他佳美的果子。"

"Let my beloved come into his garden and taste its choice fruits."
