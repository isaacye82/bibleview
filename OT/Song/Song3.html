Song of Songs 3

夜间的寻找，日间的荣耀
Night Search & Royal Procession

雅歌第3章是一幅充满张力的双联画。前半部分描述了新妇在焦虑的梦境中寻找良人，体现了爱的渴望与试炼；后半部分画风突转，展现了所罗门王从旷野而来的盛大婚礼行列，预表了基督荣耀的降临与迎娶。

Song of Songs Chapter 3 is a diptych of tension. The first half depicts the Bride's anxious dream-search for her Beloved, embodying love's longing. The scene then shifts to Solomon's majestic wedding procession from the wilderness, typifying Christ's glorious coming for His Bride.

解经与应用的方法论

Hermeneutics: Interpretation & Application

正统解经 Authoritative Exegesis

原则： 关注经文的文学结构（如梦境体裁）、历史背景（所罗门的皇家婚礼习俗）和象征语言（如“没药”、“乳香”、“刀剑”）。理解其在古代近东文学中的位置，并识别出其作为神圣盟约的比喻。

Principle: Focus on literary structure (dream genre), historical context (royal wedding customs), and symbolism. Understand it as a metaphor for the divine covenant.

灵修应用 Spiritual Application

原则： 将梦境中的寻找视为信徒对神同在的渴慕，将所罗门的婚礼视为基督迎娶教会的荣耀。强调在“属灵黑夜”中的坚持，以及对基督君王身份的尊崇。

Principle: View the dream search as the believer's longing for God's presence. See Solomon's wedding as Christ claiming His Church. Emphasize persistence in "dark nights."
