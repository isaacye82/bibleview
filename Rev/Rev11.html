Revelation 11: The Two Witnesses & The Kingdom

The Two Witnesses and the Sounding of the Seventh Trumpet

第十一章是启示录前半部分的 climax（高潮）。它从“量度圣殿”开始，引出了两个身穿毛衣的见证人，他们在敌基督的攻击下受死并复活。随着第七号的吹响，天上的大声音宣告：“世上的国成了我主和主基督的国”。这是悲伤与荣耀交织的时刻。

Chapter 11 is the climax of the first half of Revelation. It begins with the "measuring of the temple," introducing the two witnesses clothed in sackcloth, who die and rise again under the beast's attack. As the seventh trumpet sounds, a loud voice in heaven declares: "The kingdom of the world has become the kingdom of our Lord and of his Christ." A moment interwoven with sorrow and glory.

Witness & Power
见证与能力

量度圣殿 Measuring the Temple

“量度”象征神的所有权、保护和评估。神量度殿内（真信徒），但放弃外院（挂名的/外邦人），预示教会将被践踏42个月。

"Measuring" symbolizes ownership, protection, and evaluation. God measures the inner temple (true believers) but leaves the outer court (nominal/Gentiles), signaling 42 months of trampling.

两个见证人 The Two Witnesses

象征得胜的教会或先知性事工。他们具有摩西（变水为血）和以利亚（闭塞天空）的能力，身穿毛衣传道1260天。

Symbolizing the victorious church or prophetic ministry. Empowered like Moses (blood) and Elijah (drought), preaching in sackcloth for 1260 days.

第七号 (国度) 7th Trumpet (Kingdom)

这是历史的转折点。世界的政权正式移交给基督。虽然地上仍有动荡，但从天上的视角看，胜利已经确立。

The turning point of history. Worldly power is formally transferred to Christ. Though earth is tumultuous, from heaven's view, victory is established.

Methodology of Interpretation and Application

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于旧约意象（撒迦利亚书的橄榄树、以西结书的量度）和时间符号（1260天/42个月）的象征意义。
Focusing on OT imagery (Zechariah's olive trees, Ezekiel's measuring) and symbolic timing (1260 days/42 months).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    将“两个见证人”理解为教会在律法（摩西）和先知（以利亚）能力下的见证，而非仅仅是两个历史人物。量度代表属灵的保守而非物理的测量。
    Understanding "Two Witnesses" as the Church testifying in the power of Law (Moses) & Prophets (Elijah). Measuring is spiritual preservation, not physical survey.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于信徒在敌对世界中的使命：作无畏的见证人。在看似失败（被杀）时，确信最终的复活与荣耀。
Focusing on the believer's mission in a hostile world: to be fearless witnesses. Trusting in resurrection even in apparent defeat.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    虽然教会可能被践踏（外院），但真信徒（殿内）是神所珍视和保护的。我们的见证是有能力的，最终的胜利属于基督。
    Though the church may be trampled (outer court), true believers (inner temple) are measured/cherished by God. Witness is powerful; victory is Christ's.

"世上的国成了我主和主基督的国；他要作王，直到永永远远。"

"The kingdom of the world has become the kingdom of our Lord and of his Christ, and he shall reign forever and ever."
