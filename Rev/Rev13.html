Revelation 13: The Two Beasts & The Mark

邪恶的模仿：两兽的崛起与印记

The Evil Imitation: Rise of the Beasts and the Mark

第十二章的龙被摔在地上后，第十三章揭示了它在地上建立权势的手段。它召唤了两个代理人：从海中上来的兽（政治/军事强权）和从地中上来的兽（宗教/宣传迷惑）。这形成了“邪恶的三位一体”，试图通过强迫（杀害）和欺骗（假神迹）来夺取全人类的敬拜，并给他们打上666的印记。

After the Dragon is cast down in Chapter 12, Chapter 13 reveals his method of establishing power on earth. He summons two agents: the Beast from the Sea (Political/Military Power) and the Beast from the Earth (Religious/Deceptive Propaganda). This forms an "Unholy Trinity" attempting to usurp worship through coercion (killing) and deception (false miracles), marking humanity with 666.

海中上来的兽

The Beast from the Sea

Power & Blasphemy
权柄与亵渎

海兽 (The Sea Beast) Antichrist / Political Power

拥有十角七头，综合了但以理书四兽的特征。它是敌基督，通过武力、亵渎神和政治权威来统治世界。

Has 10 horns/7 heads, combining Daniel's 4 beasts. The Antichrist, ruling via force, blasphemy, and political authority.

地兽 (The Earth Beast) False Prophet / Religion

外表像羊羔（模仿基督），说话像龙（源于撒但）。它是假先知，通过假神迹迷惑人拜第一只兽。

Looks like a lamb (mimics Christ), speaks like a dragon (Satanic). The False Prophet, deceiving people to worship the first beast.

666 印记 (The Mark) Allegiance / Economy

人的数目，象征极度的不完美（缺一成七）。这是一种经济控制手段，也是对归属权的最终确认。

Number of man, symbolizing ultimate imperfection (falling short of 7). A tool of economic control and final mark of ownership.

解经与应用的方法论

Methodology of Interpretation and Application

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于识别兽的旧约原型（但以理书7章）、罗马帝国的历史背景（尼禄传说）以及末世论的终极应验。
Focusing on OT prototypes (Daniel 7), Roman historical context (Nero redivivus), and eschatological fulfillment.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    将“海兽”视为敌基督的政治体系，“地兽”视为辅助的虚假宗教体系。理解“666”为象征性的不完美，而非单纯的条形码或芯片。
    Viewing "Sea Beast" as Antichrist's political system, "Earth Beast" as false religion. Interpreting 666 as symbolic imperfection, not just a chip.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于在极权和迷惑中保持“圣徒的忍耐和信心”，以及对真神的排他性敬拜。
Focusing on maintaining "patience and faith" amidst tyranny and deception, and exclusive worship of the true God.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    警惕“像羊羔说话像龙”的欺骗。在经济压力（不能买卖）面前，坚持信仰的纯正，拒绝向世界的偶像妥协。
    Beware of deception that "looks like a lamb but speaks like a dragon". Maintain faith purity against economic pressure and worldly idols.

"圣徒的忍耐和信心就是在此。"
