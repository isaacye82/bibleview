Revelation 17: Babylon the Great & The Beast

大淫妇与朱红色的兽

The Great Harlot and the Scarlet Beast

第十七章深入揭示了“巴比伦大城”的真面目。她被描绘为一个奢华、淫乱且喝醉了圣徒之血的大淫妇，骑在朱红色的兽（政治强权）身上。这一章解开了兽的奥秘——它“先前有，如今没有，以后再有”，并预言了邪恶联盟内部的最终瓦解：兽必恨淫妇并毁灭她。

Chapter 17 unveils the true nature of "Babylon the Great". Depicted as a luxurious, immoral harlot drunk with the blood of saints, she rides a scarlet beast (political power). This chapter decodes the mystery of the beast—who "was, and is not, and will come"—and prophesies the ultimate collapse of the evil alliance: the beast will hate and destroy the harlot.

大淫妇 (The Harlot) Babylon / False Religion

身穿紫色和朱红色，以金银装饰。象征着诱惑人的世界体系、物质主义和背道的宗教系统。她用淫乱的酒使万民倾倒。

Clothed in purple/scarlet, adorned with gold. Symbolizes the seductive world system, materialism, and apostate religion. She intoxicates nations.

朱红色的兽 (Scarlet Beast) Antichrist / Political Power

遍体有亵渎的名号，七头十角。它是敌基督的政治军事力量。起初驮着淫妇（利用宗教），最后却吞吃她（毁灭宗教）。

Full of blasphemous names. The Antichrist's political/military power. Initially carries the Harlot (using religion), ultimately devours her.

得胜的羔羊 (The Lamb) Lord of Lords

万主之主，万王之王。兽与祂争战必被得胜。跟随祂的是“蒙召、被选、有忠心”的人。

Lord of lords, King of kings. The Beast wars against Him but is overcome. His followers are "called, chosen, and faithful".

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于解析象征语言：淫妇（宗教/经济体系）、兽（政治体系）、众水（多民）、七头十角（君王/国度）。
Focusing on symbolic language: Harlot (Religious/Economic), Beast (Political), Waters (Peoples), Heads/Horns (Kings).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    理解“巴比伦”为神子民的对立面（世界体系）。兽的“先前有、如今没有、以后再有”是对神永恒性的拙劣模仿。
    Understand "Babylon" as the antithesis of God's people. The Beast's timeline mimics God's eternity.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于分辨世界的诱惑，保持对基督的忠贞。
Focusing on discerning worldly seduction and maintaining loyalty to Christ.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    不仅要逃避兽的逼迫，更要警惕淫妇的诱惑（金杯）。我们的身份是“蒙召、被选、有忠心”的羔羊跟随者。
    Flee not just persecution but seduction (Golden Cup). Identify as "called, chosen, faithful" followers.

"他们与羔羊争战，羔羊必胜过他们，因为羔羊是万主之主，万王之王。"

"They will wage war against the Lamb, but the Lamb will triumph over them because he is Lord of lords and King of kings."
