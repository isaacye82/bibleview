Revelation 15: The Sea of Glass & Seven Bowls

末后大灾前的敬拜与预备

Worship and Preparation Before the Final Plagues

第十五章是启示录中最短的一章，但它充满了庄严的氛围。作为“碗”审判的序幕，它展示了天上圣殿的开启。我们看到得胜者站在玻璃海上唱“摩西之歌和羔羊之歌”，庆祝最终的救赎。随后，七位天使掌管七样末后的灾，神的荣耀（烟雾）充满圣殿，标志着审判的不可逆转。

Chapter 15 is the shortest in Revelation but filled with solemnity. As a prelude to the "Bowl" judgments, it shows the opening of the heavenly temple. Victors stand on the sea of glass singing the "Song of Moses and the Lamb," celebrating ultimate redemption. Then, seven angels receive the seven last plagues, and God's glory (smoke) fills the temple, signaling irreversible judgment.

两首凯歌 Two Songs of Victory

“摩西之歌”庆祝从法老手中得释放（出埃及），“羔羊之歌”庆祝从兽的权势下得救赎。两者结合，象征神在历史中救赎计划的完美终结。

"Song of Moses" celebrates Exodus; "Song of the Lamb" celebrates redemption from the Beast. Together, they symbolize the perfect consummation of God's redemptive plan.

七个金碗 Seven Golden Bowls

这七碗盛满了神“大怒”（Wrath）。与“号”的局部审判不同，碗的审判是全面的、没有怜悯的，为要结束地上的罪恶统治。

Filled with God's "wrath". Unlike the partial "Trumpets", the Bowls are comprehensive and without mercy, aimed at ending the reign of sin on earth.

烟雾充满 Smoke Filled the Temple

象征神荣耀和权能的临在（Shekinah）。“没有人能进殿”意味着代求的时间已过，现在是执行审判的时刻。

Symbolizes God's glory/presence (Shekinah). "No one could enter" implies the time for intercession is over; now is the time for judgment execution.

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于类型学（Typology）解读：玻璃海（红海/洗濯盆）、摩西之歌（出埃及）、烟雾（所罗门献殿/西奈山）。
Focusing on Typology: Sea of Glass (Red Sea/Laver), Song of Moses (Exodus), Smoke (Solomon's Temple/Sinai).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    将此异象视为“新出埃及”。理解“火”的双重含义：对信徒是试炼后的精金，对世界是审判的烈焰。
    View vision as "New Exodus". Dual meaning of "Fire": refined gold for believers, judgment flame for the world.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于在苦难中歌唱的能力，以及对神圣洁公义的敬畏。
Focusing on the ability to sing amidst suffering, and reverence for God's holiness and justice.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    真正的得胜不是逃避兽的攻击，而是至死忠心。在审判临到世界时，我们的回应应当是敬拜，而非恐惧。
    True victory is not escaping the Beast, but faithfulness unto death. Our response to judgment should be worship, not fear.

"主啊，谁敢不敬畏你，不将荣耀归与你的名呢？因为独有你是圣的。"

"Who will not fear you, Lord, and bring glory to your name? For you alone are holy."
