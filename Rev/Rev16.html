Revelation 16: The Seven Bowls of Wrath

神大怒的金碗与“成了”

The Bowls of God's Wrath and "It is Done"

第十六章不仅是审判的高潮，也是历史的终局。不同于号角的“三分之一”警告，七碗的审判是全面且彻底的。从恶疮到血海，从日头灼人到黑暗笼罩，神对顽梗世人的审判层层递进，直至第七碗倒在空中，宣告“成了”。这是公义的最后彰显。

Chapter 16 is not just the climax of judgment, but the end of history. Unlike the partial "one-third" warnings of the Trumpets, the Seven Bowls are comprehensive and total. From sores to seas of blood, from scorching sun to darkness, God's judgment on the unrepentant escalates until the seventh bowl is poured into the air, declaring "It is done." This is the final manifestation of justice.

倾倒的金碗

The Poured Out Bowl

Fullness of Wrath
忿怒发尽

报应 (Retribution) Blood for Blood

天使宣告神的审判是公义的：“他们曾流圣徒...的血，现在你给他们血喝。”这体现了“同态复仇法”（Lex Talionis）的神圣原则，罪有应得。

Angel declares God's judgment righteous: "They shed blood... You gave them blood to drink." Divine Lex Talionis (eye for an eye); the punishment fits the crime.

哈米吉多顿 Armageddon

希伯来话，意为“米吉多山”。象征末世善恶势力的最终集结。污秽的灵（像青蛙）用谎言聚集列国，名为与神争战，实则步入神的酒醡。

Hebrew for "Mount Megiddo". Symbolizes the final gathering of good vs evil. Unclean spirits gather nations with lies for war, but walk into God's winepress.

成了 (It is done) Gegonen

这是来自宝座的宣告，与十架上的“成了”（Tetelestai）呼应。这标志着大患难的结束，神忿怒的止息，以及巴比伦大城的彻底倾覆。

Voice from the throne, echoing "It is finished" on the Cross. Marks the end of Tribulation, the completion of Wrath, and the total collapse of Babylon.

Methodology of Interpretation and Application

权威释义

Authoritative Exegesis

侧重于与出埃及记灾难的平行对比、审判的公义性原则（报应神学），以及哈米吉多顿的末世意义。
Focusing on parallels with Exodus plagues, principles of retributive justice, and the eschatological meaning of Armageddon.

  • 核心原则 Principles
    理解金碗审判是对号角审判的升级（从1/3到全部）。确认神审判的道德基础：对杀害圣徒者的公义报应。
    Understand Bowls as escalation of Trumpets (1/3 to All). Affirm the moral basis of judgment: righteous retribution for martyred saints.

灵修默想

Spiritual Devotion

侧重于人心的顽梗（受罚却不悔改），以及在末世混乱中保持属灵警醒（看守衣服）的重要性。
Focusing on hardness of heart (suffering without repenting) and the importance of spiritual vigilance (keeping garments).

  • 核心原则 Principles
    痛苦并不自动带来悔改，若没有恩典，人只会亵渎神。信徒需在“像贼一样”的日子里保持圣洁。
    Pain doesn't guarantee repentance; without grace, man only blasphemes. Believers must remain holy in days that come "like a thief".
